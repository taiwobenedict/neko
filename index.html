Introduction

Imagine a soft, chubby cat with the confidence of a stock market guru. Maneki Neko waves its paw as if it's saying, “Hey, you! Yeah, you! Come get some of this luck!” Its raised paw isn't just for show; it's practically an antenna for good fortune, picking up signals from the universe and beaming them straight to your wallet. This purr-fessional lucky charm is ready to boost your vibes you just need to follow Neko signs.

Neko realizes that the world needs more luck and digital fortune is the way to go. The NEKO Token concept is born! In this phase, Neko assembles a team of tech-savvy cats (and maybe a few humans, but we don't know for sure).

Phase 02

With the paw-fect plan in place, it's time to hit the launch button! Maneki Neko (wearing launch goggles, of course) releases the NEKO Token into the digital wild. Early adopters grab their tokens and the lucky journey begins.

Phase 03

With NEKO out in the wild, it's time to unleash achievements! Neko expands its influence with new features, upgrades, and partnerships.

At this stage, NEKO Token is hitting headlines, dominating TikTok, and trending on every social media platform. Maneki Neko's story goes viral, with influencers, YouTubers, and content creators hopping on the #NEKOLuck wave.

01

Long ago, Maneki Neko was born in a quiet mountain village in Japan. From a young age, the little cat had a special gift: wherever it went, luck and prosperity followed. Neko helped villagers find lost coins, watched over their crops, and brought fortune to those in need. Soon, the villagers realized that their beloved Neko was no ordinary cat, but a guardian of luck.

02

With a heart full of kindness, Neko set off on a journey beyond the mountains. From Tokyo to faraway lands, Neko brought fortune to people in every corner of the world. As Neko's legend grew, more and more people wished to share in the magical luck.

03

That's when Maneki Neko had an idea. To share its gift with everyone, Neko created the NEKO Token, a digital symbol of prosperity. Through the power of the blockchain, Neko's magic was no longer limited by distance. Everyone could now hold a piece of Neko's luck in their hands.
